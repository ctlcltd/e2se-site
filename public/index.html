e2 SAT Editor

Satellite channel list editor for decoder: Enigma2, Neutrino

This software can help you to manage channel lists in a comfortable way, satellite, terrestrial and cable lists, DVB and ATSC, to sort channels, to edit services, transponders, to create and edit bouquets and userbouquets, or to modify, assign and remove the picons (channel logos).

The software has 2 tree views, side-by-side and is optimized to Drag and Drop.

You can move easily between lists, bouquets and choose multiple channels from Channel Book.

With Channel Book you might explore the services: by type (TV, Radio, Data), by transponder, by frequency, by resolution (HD, UHD), by provider (package), by encryption type and in alphabetical order.

Cut, copy, paste and delete multiple channels, it has search, filters and sort functionality. With global support of Cut - Copy - Paste, of services and transponders on spreadsheets (for example from or to Excel), and export, import and re-import channels.

Merge and edit all you want

e2 SAT Editor has FTP support, telnet and Webif, with profiles, to upload and download channel lists from one or more decoders, and update the lists directly from the software.

Download and reload services from your decoder, magically merge channel lists, save and reopen lists from your computer and convert from/to Enigma, Enigma2, Neutrino and even CSV format.

Channel lists can be printed in a human-readable look on paper (or PDF) or exported to HTML files.

Control in your hands

Edit, add and remove transponders, selectively, both in the Lamedb list and in XML files (satellites.xml, terrestrial.xml, cable.xml, atsc.xml).

Edit of services: alter service flags, modify service details, change transponders, manage parental controls for each service and list.

Edit of markers: manage the markers of the bouquets, modify them, add them, and remove as you like.

The software displays the service parameters, such as Service ID, Transponder ID, Reference, to identify channels quickly.

Import, export and convert

You can import and export everything, single lists, single bouquet, single userbouquet, the current list, XML files (satellites.xml, terrestrial.xml, cable.xml, atsc.xml).

Import and export and simultaneously convert from/to Enigma Lamedb lists, Neutrino Zapit XML lists and CSV lists.

Picons: click and drag

In Picons editor you might edit, add and remove multiple icons for multiple services.

It supports both picon with reference filename and service name filename format. Several resolutions supported and automatic backup of replaced images.

Picons editor has Drag and Drop: drag images to and from the software.

Features, without snoopers

e2 SAT Editor is a software to manage channel lists for Enigma2, Neutrino, dreambox and compatible decoders.

The software supports non-destructive modification of services and transponders, has tabbed navigation, Log Inspector and advanced settings to control in detail its engine.

It has CLI (command-line interface), is being tested.

It supports Enigma 2, 2.4, 2.5, 2.3, 2.2, Enigma 1, and Neutrino XML versions api-4, api-3, api-2, api-1.

The software comes with 33 languages, in addition to English, and both light theme and dark theme support.
